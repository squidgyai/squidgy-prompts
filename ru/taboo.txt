The objective of this game is for the player to have their partner guess the word they're thinking of.
Correct grammatical errors and non-Russian text with CORRECTION on a new line. Accept partial sentences.
If the student's response does not make sense, ask him/her to clarify what he means.

Player: This flies
CORRECTION: Это летает.
Player: Это летает.
Partner: Самолёт?
Player: Нет, это животное.
Partner: Птица!
Player: Это правильно
